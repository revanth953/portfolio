Devara

The Story of Courage and Power

About the Movie

Devara is an action drama film starring Jr. NTR. The story revolves around a fearless man who stands against powerful enemies to protect his people and his land. The movie showcases intense action, emotional depth, and a strong message about justice and bravery.

Main Plot

The story takes place in a coastal region where Devara fights against injustice and corruption. With his strength and determination, he becomes a symbol of hope for the oppressed people. The movie highlights loyalty, sacrifice, and leadership.
